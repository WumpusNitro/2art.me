Cloudy

My beautiful yet work in progress OC and truesona as well. The most amount of Blue you will see on my website.

The lore.

You may ask yourself. Where did Cloudy come from? Well he was born from the Zero Point (Not from Fortnite) and got teleported in some dark world where Cloudy was the only one alive but has a bit of a superpower to teleport everywhere.

But Cloudy likes cuddles and always wants to help people out and socialize! Either it's just to talk about a topic to needing to vent. Cloudy will hear you out about it and support you no matter what. He's a great companion.

Cloudy likes to hang out in clouds when he's bored.
