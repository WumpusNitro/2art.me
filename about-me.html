My Intrests

 Trains and Subways (Metros)

I like trains. Almost everyone knows this short phrase. But i am an avid fan of trains since i was 3 years old. I still remember what train i saw first. That being a Z50000 Train a French Train (duh) but since then i have been around Train/Freedrive Communities such as Potatocorp (PTA) and many more! I even made a Train Concept at one point. If you are a Train Nerd as well. Here are a list of my favorite trains with a short description of why i like those trains a lot.

Oh and by the way. That Train Concept can be found here (I will need to create a whole new project page for this) | if you see this. This is coming soon :3

Toronto Rocket (TR)

 Location | Toronto - Canada

 Rating 8.2/10

When i first came to Toronto (and not for a flight layover or something) just to ride on the subways (and to have fun obviously) i fell in love with the Toronto Rocket. Sure, It's just a train with aluminum alloy and not much else to it but the Text to Speech voice gave me a nostalgia feeling. Even though i never lived in Toronto. So much in fact that i found the original TTS on the Internet. The interior is great too.

MR-73

 Location | Montréal - Canada

 Rating 7.8/10

If you a transport foamer that knows a little bit about Montréal's metro system. You will know that the motor sounds on this baby is amazing.
Take a listen but apart of that. It's an old train but the vibe is amazing.
